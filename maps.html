DUSTBOWL

One of Team Fortress 2's first maps. It's a control point map, every two control points captured by the BLU team, the map switches over to a different section of the map. The full amount of points BLU can capture is six. This is a test to see if downloading maps is possible. Try it out yourself if you want to! :)
